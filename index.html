Driver Assistance

Safety that
never blinks.

EyeSight uses dual cameras to monitor traffic and road conditions — reacting faster than human reflexes to keep everyone inside safe.

01

Pre-Collision Braking

Automatically brakes when a collision with a vehicle, pedestrian, or cyclist is imminent — day or night.

02

Adaptive Cruise Control

Maintains a safe following distance automatically, slowing and accelerating with traffic flow.

03

Lane Centering Assist

Gently steers to keep the vehicle centred in the lane, reducing driver fatigue on long journeys.

Monitors driver alertness with facial recognition, issuing warnings before drowsiness leads to danger.
